Articles for the Climate Change Club

We love sharing important articles about climate change and its effects with the world!

One of our members recently visited Japan. When she visited Suma Beach, she experienced a surprising sight. She saw a fish made completely out of plastic! She is still gathering information, and one thing she learned was that Suma Beach is known for being clean and well maintained. The Climate Change Club has a hypothisis that workers at Suma Beach collected trash from nearby cities and/or oceans and made it into a plastic fish as a sign of cleanness or a small peaceful protest.

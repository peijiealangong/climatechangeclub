Article: Egg

We love sharing important articles about climate change and its effects with the world!

An egg was found on the blacktop of ACE's playground, an unexpected sight. There were no trees nearby, making it impossiable for it to have fallen from a nest, except for if it was accidently interfered with by kids.

While the members of the Climate Change Club are still investigating this scene, a new theory has been developed: the egg would have shattered if it fell from a height. So, an interference may be the case, wether a person or animal. Let us know what you think in the Contact Us page.

Although there have been serveral theorys made, none have been provin true yet. We will update this article once we have more information.

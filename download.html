🏆 Egg Catcher Scoring System

Master the game by understanding the scoring mechanics:

Golden Eggs (⭐)

Standard eggs worth 10 points each. These are your most common catch during gameplay.

Bonus Eggs (✨)

Special eggs worth 25 points! Catch these for a significant score boost and combo multiplier.

Power-ups (🔥)

Temporary abilities like shields, multipliers, or speed boosts. Use these strategically!

Combo Multiplier

Catch eggs consecutively to build combos. Your multiplier increases with each successive catch!

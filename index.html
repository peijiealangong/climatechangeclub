🌍 Welcome to the Climate Change Club

Activism, sustainability, and student-powered change.

🌱 Our Mission

We combat climate change through community projects, education, and direct action.

🛠️ Our Approach

We prioritize prevention over cleanup—addressing root causes before symptoms.

🔥 Climate Change: Our Challenge, Our Opportunity

  • Extreme Weather: Hurricanes, wildfires, floods, and droughts are intensifying.
  • Rising Temperatures: Over 1°C increase since pre-industrial times.
  • Environmental Injustice: Marginalized communities bear the brunt.

💡 What You Can Do

  • Learn: Attend workshops and speaker events.
  • Act: Join sustainability initiatives and advocate for green policies.
  • Create: Use writing, art, or media to inspire change.

✊ Join the Movement: The Climate Change Club is a student-powered force for the future.

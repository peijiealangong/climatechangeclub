About the Climate Change Club

The Climate Change Club is a student‑powered community focused on sustainability, environmental awareness, and real‑world action. We host events, run campaigns, publish resources, and collaborate with local organizations to make climate literacy and climate action part of everyday life.

This experimental UI is designed to make our mission easier to explore: clearer sections, more visual hierarchy, and subtle motion that guides your eye without overwhelming you.

  • Education: Workshops, articles, and presentations about climate science and solutions.
  • Action: School‑wide initiatives, local clean‑ups, and advocacy projects.
  • Community: A welcoming space for anyone curious about climate issues.
  • Creativity: Posters, zines, digital media, and student‑led campaigns.

UI Experiment Panel

Below are some of the visual and interaction experiments we’re testing on this page. Think of them as building blocks we might reuse across the site. Try hovering, scrolling, and reading carefully—then tell us what feels good and what feels distracting.

✨ Glass Panels

Soft, frosted containers that separate content without heavy borders.

🌓 Dark Mode Awareness

Colors and shadows that adapt when dark mode is enabled on the site.

🎨 Gradient Typography

Gradient‑filled headings that highlight important sections without using images.

⚡ Motion on Hover

Cards that lift, glow, or shift slightly when you interact with them.

🌐 Orbiting Backgrounds

Subtle animated shapes behind the content to add depth and energy.

📚 Dense but Scannable Text

Longer explanations broken into sections so you can skim or dive deep.

How This UI Might Roll Out

Nothing on this page is final. Think of it as a sketchbook that happens to be interactive. If people like this direction, we’ll gradually bring pieces of it into the main site.

Phase 1 — Feedback Collect reactions from beta members and club officers.
Phase 2 — Refine Keep the best ideas, remove anything that feels distracting.
Phase 3 — Integrate Apply the chosen patterns to the About, Projects, and Events pages.
Phase 4 — Launch Ship a polished v1.4 UI to everyone visiting the site.

FAQ (For This UI Test)

Is this the final design? +
No. This is a preview environment where we can try ideas quickly. Some parts will change, and some may never reach the main site.
What kind of feedback is most helpful? +
Tell us what feels clear, what feels confusing, what’s fun, and what’s annoying. Specific comments like “this card is too bright” or “this section is my favorite” are incredibly useful.
Will dark mode stay? +
Almost certainly yes. This page is testing how well our components adapt to both light and dark themes without needing separate designs.
Can I suggest my own UI ideas? +
Absolutely. If you have layout sketches, color ideas, or animation concepts, share them in the beta feedback form or directly with the club leaders.

Trees aren't just "regular" now, they're extraordinary

Trees! A CO2 reducer, and now an eye-catcher.

Recently, one of our members visited Vancouver. There, they saw strange trees. Trees that were cut in a zig zag pattern! Trees that were growing out of trees! First of all, people want to mimic wildlife. they cut the trees at varying points, making it seem more natural. Sometimes, they shave the trees in wavy directions, mimicking how a beaver would chew a thin log. And, though not very likely, trees can grow out of the stumps! See some pictures below. Second, people want to eliminate the fire hazard as much as possible. Our member does not find this likely, however, since they leave tall stumps and long fallen trunks without bringing them to a safer area. Lastly, people do it for the safety of both the citizens and the tree. Though they are cutting the trees down, they only target already dead, diseased, or crowded areas where it's best for the tree to be cut down. In conclusion, people cut down trees for the best of everyone, giving habitats to wildlife, making wildfires less likely, and helping other trees develop. *We will update this article and write more about Vancouver soon!
